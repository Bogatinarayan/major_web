About the Project

Jarvis AI is a sophisticated voice-controlled personal assistant developed as a major project during our 6th semester in Computer Engineering. The system is designed to assist users with daily tasks through natural language processing, enabling features such as voice recognition, executing commands, web browsing, playing music, and managing schedules. The project demonstrates our team's expertise in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software development create by droid group.
